What is Frontline Futures?

Frontline Futures is an opportunity for students to learn about the life of a medical professional and the path to working in STEM. Listen to doctors speak about their paths to success, hear from current medical, dental and pharmaceutical students about their experiences at different universities and enter a raffle to win prizes!



Why & Who Can Attend?

This event is open to all students across Ontario, from high school through university! Medical professionals will be available to answer all your questions about the field of STEM, no matter your level of experience.
You'll be making yourself a more competitive applicant to the field of medicine, and 100% of the proceeds go towards buying protective equipment for Ottawa's frontline workers.



When & Where + How Can I Participate?

Frontline Futures will be a 3 hour conference on Zoom from 1:30 to 4:30 pm on Saturday July 4th. The cost to participate is $8 per ticket, on sale until July 1st! All proceeds go towards buying supplies for Ottawa hospitals.
